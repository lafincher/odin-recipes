Honey's Cornbread

Description

My Honey makes the best cornbread on either side of the Mississippi! Baked in a cast iron skillet, the edges and tops are cripsy and the inside is hardy but fluffy enough to absorb butter, collard green juices, chili, or whatever it's served with!

Make this cornbread when you wanna feel like you're bein hugged by your grandma.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups corn meal
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups runny low fat buttermilk
  • ~2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Grease cast iron skillet with olive oil
  3. Combine corn meal, egg, and buttermilk
  4. Pour into greased cast iron skillet
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the edges are pulling from the sides
  6. Serve with softened butter

Add cheddar cheese, jalapenos, and corn kernals for a TexMex version
