Satisfying Shake

Description

I had this flavor of shake for the first time at a Jamba Juice in the Las Vegas Airport. It hit all the spots and more!

Peanut butter, chocolate, bananas. Truly a match made in heaven.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 1 banana, sliced (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 dates (soaked in 1 tbsp water for easier blending but not necessary)
  • 2 tsp cocao powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup oat milk, more as needed
  • add ice to desired thickness

Steps

  1. In a blender, add ingredients listed above
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Add additional oat milk or ice to desire consistency
  4. Drink right out of the blender or serve yourself in a cute glass
  5. Enjoy the heavenly treat

Add 2 tsp of instant coffee or espresso powder for extra energy!
