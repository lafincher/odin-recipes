Tuna Salad

Description

Growing up, I never considered tuna salad as fish. That was weird! I love tuna salad so much I could eat it every day.

I like to pair my tuna with crackers, slices of cheese, and sliced pickles to make a stack of yum but it's just as good between two slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tuna, in water
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 stalk celery, diced
  • 2 tbsp dill relish, or more, to taste
  • 1/4 cup Duke's mayo
  • 2 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp Spike Seasoning
  • 1 tsp fresh cracked black pepper
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Drain cans of tuna
  2. Add all ingredients to a bowl
  3. Stir to combine
  4. Taste and add anything you want more of
  5. Serve with crackers or on a sandwich

Spike Seasoning is the secret ingredient in this one!
